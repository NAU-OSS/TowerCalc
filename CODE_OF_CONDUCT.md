# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in this community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

We are committed to fostering an open, welcoming, and collaborative environment for all contributors regardless of:

- Experience level
- Gender identity and expression
- Sexual orientation
- Disability
- Personal appearance
- Race, ethnicity, or nationality
- Religion
- Age

This project exists to promote learning, collaboration, and transparent theorycrafting for Anime World Tower Defense. Respect and professionalism are essential to achieving that goal.

---

## Our Values

We value:

- Transparency in calculations and design decisions
- Constructive feedback and collaboration
- Respectful technical discussion
- Continuous learning and improvement
- Community-driven development

---

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

- Be respectful and considerate in communication
- Provide constructive feedback
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on technical merit rather than personal criticism
- Clearly explain reasoning when proposing changes
- Help create a positive and welcoming environment

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Public or private harassment
- Doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- Repeatedly posting off-topic content
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. Open a private issue labeled **Code of Conduct Report**, or
2. Contact the repository maintainer directly via GitHub.

Please include:

- A description of the incident
- Links or screenshots if applicable
- Any relevant context

Reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Possible enforcement actions include:

1. **Correction** – A private warning for minor violations.
2. **Warning** – A formal warning with expectations for improvement.
3. **Temporary Ban** – Temporary restriction from contributing.
4. **Permanent Ban** – Removal from the community for severe or repeated violations.

Enforcement decisions will be made fairly and based on the severity and pattern of behavior.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Project documentation
- Any project-related communication
